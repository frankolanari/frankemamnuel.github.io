Ford GoBike Data Exploration

Tools: Python (Pandas, Numpy, Seaborn, Matplotlib).

In this Ford GoBike System dataset analysis,I employed Python, Jupyter Notebook, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn to investigate bike ride patterns in the San Francisco Bay area. Through univariate, bivariate, and multivariate analyses, key insights were uncovered. These include Thursdays as the busiest day, longer weekend rides, and gender-based trip duration differences. Our findings offer valuable input for bike-sharing service improvements and infrastructure enhancements, with potential for further exploration of demographics and weather effects.
